Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start?

I can usually schedule your service within 2-3 days of your request, depending on current bookings and weather conditions.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept cash, check, or Venmo. Payment is due upon completion of service.

Do I need to be home during service?

Not necessarily! As long as I have access to the areas that need service, you don't need to be home. We can arrange details when scheduling.

What if it rains?

Safety first! If weather conditions aren't suitable, I'll reschedule your service for the next available day.
